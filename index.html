How We Do it?
Awareness Drive
Through educational programs, seminars, and workshops, we aim to promote responsible animal ownership and raise awareness about stray animal welfare.
Public Policy Shift
Advocating for policy change with the help of bureaucrats, government officials, Public Representative, and Policymaker to ensure better protection and care for all animals.
Joint Efforts
Our joint efforts include adoption drives, vaccination camps, and public awareness programs to build a animal friendly city with the help of NGOs and different organisation.
Providing Resources
Providing essential resources for animals, ensuring their well-being and safety. From food and shelter to medical care, we strive to support stray and rescued animals.
